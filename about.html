Who am I?

Hello family! My name is Carolina Albuquerque. I am... I am. Divine healer, light & energy worker, a practitioner, a student, a friend, a family member, someone who is passionate about growing in community. It would be my honor to accompany you in your healing journey.

My story

I began my journey of healing at thirteen when I developed a serious shoulder injury from over training. I was a competitive swimmer from a very young age, eventually stopping due to my shoulder injury. I attributed my sense of self, safety, and belonging to success in the pool. Hello root chakra imbalance! My wonderful coach and founder of my team suggested I go to yoga for additional shoulder support. I was resistant at the beginning. I found the pace of practice, the focus on breathing, the stretching almost unbearable. The practice did not match the pace of my fast chaotic speedy mind. I struggled to connect, and for some reason I kept going back. Over time, with continued practice and integration, I developed an ever evolving love for Yoga, breath work, meditation, Bhakti, and much more. I have been practicing yoga for over a decade. I have taken a variety of training in Yin YTT (50+ hrs), Ayurvedic Coach & Ayurveda training (75+ hrs), Vinyasa YTT (200+ hrs), Holy Fire Reiki level I and II and more! I am currently taking a year-long advanced Ayurveda course with the Shakti School as well as a 7 week course: Redesigning the Self, the Road to Transcendence with Muktananda. I am also finishing my Bachelors degree at Boston College. I love to learn. I am always jumping on more opportunities to learn wherever I can. In the future I look to continue my journey learning and connecting, studying to become a Tantric Priestess, and eventually starting a healing center. I am a modern day light healer. I work with beings to help them discover their genuine most powerful selves. Through energy work: reiki, individual nutrition coaching, and breath work I help your body alchemize, move energy, and enter deeply into the safety of your skin. My early years involved sexual trauma, family trauma, and an unstable life of addition and disacossiation. I was diagnosed with complex PTSD, anxiety, and depression in high school and found myself causing chaos wherever I went. I share this because, as a modern day healer, I hope to be relatable to you. I hope you see yourself in me. I am Portuguese American. I currently live in South Florida. Thank you for being a miracle and finding your way to this sanctuary of healing. I'd love to get to know you deeper.
